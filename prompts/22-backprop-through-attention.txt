Create a React component visualizing backpropagation through an attention layer.

Key insight: Gradients flow backward through softmax, through QK^T, through the linear projections. Understanding this flow is crucial for understanding training dynamics.

Requirements:
1. Forward pass: show activations flowing through Q, K, V projections → attention → output
2. Backward pass: show gradients flowing in reverse
3. Color intensity = gradient magnitude
4. Highlight the softmax Jacobian (where most complexity is)
5. Show gradient flow to all three projections W_Q, W_K, W_V
6. Interactive: hover over any edge to see the gradient formula

The "aha": why training attention is harder than training MLPs.

Colors: forward in teal (#14b8a6), backward in orange (#f59e0b), high gradient = bright.
Background: #080c14

Output TypeScript/TSX for Next.js with animated SVG.
