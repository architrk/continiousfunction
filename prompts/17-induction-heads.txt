Create a React component visualizing Induction Heads - the fundamental circuit for in-context learning.

Key insight: Induction heads implement pattern completion: "I saw [A][B] before. I see [A] now. Predict [B]."

Two-head circuit:
1. Previous Token Head: copies info from position t-1 to t
2. Induction Head: looks for previous occurrence of current token, attends to what followed

Requirements:
1. Show a sequence like "Harry Potter... Harry [?]"
2. Visualize the two attention patterns as connection diagrams
3. Step through the circuit: (1) find "Harry" earlier, (2) look at what followed, (3) predict "Potter"
4. Show attention scores as line thickness
5. Interactive: input different repeated patterns, watch the circuit activate

This is THE core mechanism for in-context learning.

Colors: previous token head in teal (#14b8a6), induction head in orange (#f59e0b).
