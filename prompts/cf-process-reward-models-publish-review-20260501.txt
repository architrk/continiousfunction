The page teaches:
- outcome supervision as a terminal label on a complete trace
- process supervision as a local label on a prefix plus next step
- a Bernoulli step verifier trained with cross-entropy
- additive step-logit trajectory scoring
- KL-regularized reweighting over complete traces, matching the RLHF pattern
- the limitation that PRMs are still learned proxies and can be verifier-hacked

Interactive demo:
- fixed finite reasoning traces for 2(x+3)=14
- default process verifier selects the clean derivation
- outcome-only verifier selects a lucky final answer with invalid steps
- false-positive process verifier error makes the process verifier select the lucky invalid trace
- controls: scorer, aggregation, verifier error, beta
- metrics: selected trace, expected terminal correctness, expected step correctness, expected verifier score, selected proxy error, KL(policy || ref)
