"Temperature is high, but top-k = 1. What happens to diversity?"

Intended teaching point:
- Temperature reshapes probabilities before filtering.
- Top-k=1 truncates the candidate set to one token, so diversity is gone regardless of temperature.
- Top-p may further truncate but is not the binding constraint when top-k=1.
- Raw logits can remain the same while the sampling distribution changes.
