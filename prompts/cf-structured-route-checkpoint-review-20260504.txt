Project: Continuous Function, a static-export-friendly Next.js learning atlas.

Product goal:
The site should preserve a learner's current question and the smallest useful checkpoint across paper mapper, graph, home, and the attention-serving lab. This should feel continuous and useful without becoming creepy or noisy. Browser `localStorage` is the right early-stage constraint.

Prior Oracle guidance already accepted:
- Do not build accounts/cloud saved paths yet.
- Keep one compact local route snapshot under `cf:last-learning-route`.
- Store the paper/clue separately from the mapped route title.
- Preserve grounding status.
- Store only a small lastObservation, not a full activity log.
- For the KV checkpoint, remember enough structured lab state to restore the exact experiment.
- Show what changed, what stayed fixed, the result, the caveat, and the next question.
- Ignore malformed or oversized localStorage values.

What I changed in this pass:
- Extended `LearningRouteSnapshot.lastObservation` with optional structured fields:
  - `kind: 'formula-comparison'`
  - `changed: { symbol, from, to }`
  - `heldFixed: [{ symbol, value }]`
  - `result: { before, after, ratio, unit }`
  - `caveat`
- Hardened snapshot validation with bounded strings/arrays for top-level snapshot fields, route concepts, equations, observations, held-fixed values, and numeric result fields.
- Updated the KV checkpoint in `AttentionServingModule` to save the structured formula comparison:
  - `H_kv 32 -> 8`
  - held fixed: `B`, `T`, `N_layers`, `d_head`, precision
  - result in decimal GB
  - caveat excluding allocator/paged-cache/metadata/scheduler/layout overhead
  - lab state for restoring sliders.
- Updated home and graph resume surfaces to render the held-fixed assumptions and caveat.
