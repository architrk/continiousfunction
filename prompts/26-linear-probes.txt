Create a React component visualizing linear probes and the Linear Representation Hypothesis.

Key insight: Concepts like "truthfulness" or "sentiment" are encoded as directions in activation space. A linear probe (single layer) can read them out.

Requirements:
1. 2D activation space with points representing different inputs
2. A "concept direction" as an arrow
3. Points colored by how much they align with the direction
4. Show projection: dot product with direction = concept score
5. Interactive: drag the direction, see classifications change
6. Example: "positive/negative sentiment" direction separates reviews

The word vector analogy: King - Man + Woman ≈ Queen visualized.

Colors: positive examples in teal, negative in red, direction in orange.
Background: #0d1219
