We are building Continuous Function, a Next.js/static-export-friendly learning platform for frontier AI papers, deep learning math, interactive concept notebooks, paper mapping, graph routes, equation objects, toy labs, and source-grounded AI companion prompts.

Current product direction:
- The site should become a research-learning operating system where concept, equation, paper, visualization, toy experiment, claim, and misconception objects can each carry a discussion thread.
- We are early-stage, so do not design accounts, auth, full backend, notifications, moderation, billing, or real threaded comments yet.
- We need a narrow first implementation that preserves object identity and makes future discussion infrastructure easy to attach.

Relevant current state:
- `/paper-map` already renders equation objects, source boxes, claim checks, toy-lab specs, and disabled discussion prompts.

- `/paths/attention-serving` already has discussion prompt cards attached to exact claims.
- Concept notebooks already have section-level AI actions and practice shells, but no object-attached discussion surface.
- We need to keep static export safe and local-only for this checkpoint.
