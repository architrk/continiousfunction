We are building Continuous Function, a Next.js/React learning platform for deep mathematical ideas. The product goal is to make learning joyful, rigorous, visual, interactive, and worth returning to. We use GPT Pro/Oracle extensively as a design/review partner, then verify locally.

This is the implementation review for the RAG notebook-shell rollout slice selected by GPT Pro/Oracle after SSM Hybrids.

Oracle selected this slice with the central learner action:
- Predict whether the RAG system has no current evidence, retrieves the current evidence but still answers wrongly, or retrieves enough fresh evidence mass to answer correctly.
- Reveal should show the top-k set, scores/weights, evidence mass, answer mixture, selected answer, cited source, citation support, and correctness.
- The invariant must remain narrow: coverage, evidence mass, answer correctness, citation support, and freshness are separate quantities.
