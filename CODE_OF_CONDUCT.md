# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project is for patient, respectful, technically serious collaboration.

Examples of behavior that help:

- asking good-faith questions
- giving specific, actionable feedback
- disagreeing with ideas without attacking people
- helping newer contributors understand design and math choices

Examples of behavior that are not acceptable:

- harassment or personal attacks
- dismissive or demeaning language
- bad-faith arguing, trolling, or deliberate disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior makes collaboration worse or unsafe.

If you need a private escalation path, use the guidance in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or request a private contact channel through the repository.
